Why these six

I've sat through enough discovery workshops to notice the pattern: every manufacturer I work with has the same six flows. Names change. Departments change. The shape doesn't. So I keep these as blank scaffolds and walk into Day 1 with them on a wall.

What you get below isn't a finished BPMN diagram — it's a starting prompt. The lanes are right. The decision points are right. The actors are wrong on purpose, so the room argues productively about who owns what.
